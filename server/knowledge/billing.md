# Billing

## Plans

We offer Starter ($9/month), Pro ($29/month), and Team ($79/month). All plans include unlimited chat support and email support.

## Payment methods

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal. Invoices are available on Team plans.

## Refunds

Monthly subscriptions can be refunded within 14 days of the first charge if you have not exceeded 1,000 API calls. Contact support with your account email.

## Canceling

You can cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. Access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not prorate mid-cycle cancellations.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, we retry for 7 days. After that, the account is paused (read-only) until payment is updated.
