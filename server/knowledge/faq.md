# FAQ

## What is this product?

We are a SaaS platform for customer support automation: chat widget, knowledge base, and integrations with Slack and Zendesk.

## Password reset

Click **Forgot password** on the login page. Reset links expire after **1 hour**. If you do not receive email, check spam or contact support.

## Data privacy

We store data in **US and EU** regions. You can request data export or deletion under **Settings → Privacy**. GDPR requests are processed within 30 days.

## Uptime

Our status page is at `https://status.example.com`. We target **99.9%** monthly uptime on paid plans.

## Escalation to humans

If the assistant cannot help, users can click **Talk to a human** to open a ticket. Tickets are answered within 4 hours on Pro, 1 hour on Team.
